# Rules

## Components
- 104 Number cards (1-13). 8 cards each of number
- 5 joker cards
- First player card (Paw)

## Setup
### 3-5 players
- First player take "first player card"
- 11 cards per player
- 
### 2 players
- First player take "first player card"
- 11 cards per player
- 2 joker cards per player
