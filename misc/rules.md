# Rules

## Components
- 104 Number cards (1-13). 8 cards each of number
- 5 joker cards
- Active player card (Paw)

## Setup
### 3-5 players
- First player take "active player card"
- 11 cards per player
- Reveal 6 cards in pool section (public cards)
- Draw deck
- Discard pile 	
### 2 players
- First player take "active player card"
- 11 cards per player
- 2 joker cards per player
- Reveal 13 cards in pool section (public cards)
- Draw deck
- Discard pile

## Gameplay
- Active player take paw card
- Play clockwise
